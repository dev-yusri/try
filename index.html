Frequently Asked Questions

What is GTU?

GTU stands for GoTechUp, the company that manages the game development process.

How do I access the game tracker?

You can access the GTU Tracker by logging into the platform with your credentials.

What is the difference between GTU and i2loc?

i2loc is a tutorial term localization system, while GTU focuses on game development and tracking.
